Welcome to Pro Nails!

Hello everyone!

Finding a excellent nail salon to rejuvenate and pamper your nails and your body is a sign of expressing yourself. Come to Pro Nails Salon. This salon is a place where clients should feel relax, comfortable, and be served by the excellent hands in Eugene. We gurantee that your visit to our nail salon will give you the best service.

We offer variety of services to pamper your hands and feets as well as to rejuvenate your beauty with our special nail and spa treatmeants We are skillful and professional nail technicians. We will always try the best to make each of your visits the most wonderful and pleasant. Especially, sanitation is always our first priority to ensure your health and safety. All of our nail instruments, manicure and pedicure stations are sterilized, sanitized, disinfected after every use following Occupational Safety and Health Administration.

We are located in Springfield, OR. We have technicians with many years of experience. We like to take pride in doing a great job in the nail & beauty care industry.

Come and visit Pro Nails Salon, where you deserve to be beautiful!
